About EDC HITAM

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) was established at HITAM in 2018.

EDC operates under the HITAM Innovation and Incubation Centre (HIIC), the official incubator of HITAM, located on the first floor of the Activity Block.

EDC focuses on developing an entrepreneurial mindset among students and encourages them to think beyond traditional career paths.

EDC helps students convert their ideas into startups by providing proper guidance, mentorship, and support.

Vision Blueprint

Vision

To build an inclusive entrepreneurial ecosystem that empowers students to innovate fearlessly, develop sustainable ventures and emerge as socially responsible leaders driving global impact.

Action Vectors

Mission

  • • Skill Development: Provide opportunities for learning through workshops, seminars and competitions.
  • • Incubation & Venture Creation: Facilitate creation of at least 5 student startups annually.
  • • Industry Partnerships: Build partnerships with industrial and regional ecosystems.
  • • Sustainable Impact: Encourage students to build sustainable business metrics.
